Safety And Guidelines

We at Fluxster make user safety our top priority while also having a useful and fun community to be a part of. To ensure we have this community like this, we have ground rules that every user must follow. Guidelines are from our Terms of service and our Privacy Policy

  • Do...
  • Be wise what you share - Whatever you post on Fluxster can be seen publicly on the web.*
  • Choose the right privacy settings - Be sure to change your privacy settings that will make you feel comfortable on Fluxster.
  • Report inappropriate content - If you come across content that is not suitable on Fluxster, do not hesitate to report it.
  • Do not...
  • Abuse your rights - Being a user on Fluxster is a privilege, and those who abuse guidelines will have their accounts taken away.
  • Inappropriate or pornagraphic meterial - Any inappropriate** or pornagraphic meterial in posts, profile information, or profile design will lead to account termination.
  • Be rude and inappropriate - Be respectful to other users on Fluxster. Any cases of inappropriate or rude behavior may lead to account termination.
  • Sharing Password - In no way should you share your password or any other data to anyone on Fluxster. Even when it comes to family or friends.

* This still applies if your privacy settings are set to only followers having access your posts.
** See the Terms of Service (Posts and Private Messaging).

What if I have a safety concern?
If you have concerns regarding your safety, privacy or have come across inappropriate content on Fluxster, you can contact us by emailing at contact@fluxster.net

Badges


What are badges?

Badges are a colored text shown on user profiles to represent a title. Like: Staff or Official

These badges can be helpful to users like businesses or creators.

Tthe "Official" badge can help users know that the person or business that they are following is the real person or business, and not a imposter account.

The "Creator" badge can help identify a creator user. Example: Musician, Game Developer, Artist, etc.

Users that have badges with red text, like "Developer", are members of the Fluxster Staff team. This can also be identified with the badge with blue text like "Staff".

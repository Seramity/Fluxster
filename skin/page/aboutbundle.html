Want to post your content to a specific list of people? Do you want to have an organized group within Fluxster? Bundle is a great tool for that!
Bundle is a group-like client that can help users collect a group of people and share content, post messages, and post news to other members within the bundle. Bundle is perfect for events, fan pages, and much more.

Features included

Share Content

Post messages within your bundle for your members to see.

Privacy

Whether you want an open, private, or closed bundle, you can pick either setting to your preference.

Post News

Posting news can help you post news, info, etc. that will be featured on your bundle for your members to see.

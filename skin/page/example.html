What is Fluxster?

Fluxster is a site that allows web developers and designers promote and gain more traffic to their website flawlessly. Users can submit sites and visit, favorite, and support other sites. Everything is free. So don't worry.

Why join?

Weither you own a small site or a complex social network, it is nice to have many, many people using your website. Instead of having it collect dust.

How do I get Started?

If you want to look around Fluxster a bit, just click the "Visit Fluxster" button at the top of the page. If you want to create a Fluxster account, click the "Create a account" button at the top of the page. If you'd like to see our Facebook page, click the "Facebook page" button at the top.

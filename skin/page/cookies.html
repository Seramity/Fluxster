Cookies

Fluxster uses cookies in order to ensure better, faster, and a more secure experience possible.

What are cookies?
Cookies are small bit of data that is saved on to your browser. Which the website can refer back to the data stored.

What does Fluxster use cookies for?
Fluxster uses cookies in order to save user data, like username and password. We do this so we know that the infomation is right for you to access your account.

We do use third party applications on Fluxster which may store Third Party Cookies. None of your "personal" infomation will be sent to these third party applications. (This was explained in the policy popup when you first visit Fluxster)

The third party applications that may store third party cookies on your browser:

• Gravatar
• Google Adsense
• Google Analytics

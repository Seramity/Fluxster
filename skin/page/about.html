What is Fluxster?
Well, it's quite simple. It's a social networking site. Made by the same guy (Nick) who made Socialblock and the never released Avedoo.
Fluxster is based off of the concept of the app "Post-it" that was in Socialblock. Which allowed you to post comments and media for everyone on Socialblock to see.

Since the start of development in September 2012, we have been trying our best to create a new experience that Socialblock never had. And with development of Avedoo was never finished and was later canceled, we felt like it was needed it actually make another site and finish it.

What's so special about Fluxster?
When Socialblock was made, we wanted a little community on Socialblock
And now with a customized server and engine, we can do way much more to create a kickass experience. Practically a 10x better Socialblock.

What is Socialblock?
Socialblock was also a social networking site made and hosted off of a website maker called Webs.
Socialblock had the same concept that Fluxster has. It was made for people to share and make friends. But Socialblock wasn't very successful but did hold a small community. Which me hope to build a bigger one on Fluxster.

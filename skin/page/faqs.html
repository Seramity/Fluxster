Account

How do I change my settings?

Everything from your name to social media links can be found and changed all here.

How do I change my profile picture?

You can upload a profile picture here. Though if you have a Gravatar account and you used the same email with Gravatar and Fluxster, your profile picture will be already updated.

How do I change my password?

You can easily change the password to your account here. If you have forgotten you password, you can send an email to change it here.

I forgot my password

If you have forgotten you password, you can send an email to change it here.

I want to delete my account

If you feel that you no longer want to use your account, you can delete it here. Note that all your posts, account settings, and other infomation stored on Fluxster will be permanently deleted and will not be retrievable.

I want to deactivate my account

Instead of permanently deleting your account and all its infomation, if you just want to take a break from Fluxster you can simply deactivate it.
You can do it here, on the same page to delete your account. Just be sure to press hte "Disable" button instead. When you want your account back, whenever you log back in, you will be redirected to a activate account page.

How do I make my profile private?

If you want to make your profile private, you can change it in the settings page.

In the settings page, there is an option in the "Design" section where you can either display or hide your badges on your profile.

Posting

How do I post?

When you are logged in, you can easily post a message on the Stream page.

How do I delete my post?

Anywhere on pages like on the Stream or your profile, you can find an option " Delete" below the message. When you click it, your post will be permanently deleted.

@mentions

If you want to post to or make a post about a user, you can use a mention tag.
Examples: @fluxster, @nick, @dinosaur

#Hashtags

Topics on Fluxster are handled by hashtags. Want your post to be within a topic, just include a hashtag.
Example: #summer, #morning, #fluxster

Reporting a Post

If you find a post on Fluxster you find that is not appropriate, you can report it to be removed by pressing at the bottom right of the post.

Other

Why do I get redirected to a "Private Account" page?

If you are trying to visit a users profile page, and their settings are set to either "Followings only" or "Completely Private", you will be redirected to that page.

If their settings are set to "Followings only", they would have to be following you in order for you to see their profile and have the ability to follow them.

If their settings are set to "Completely private", no matter if they are following you or not, no one can visit their profile or follow them.

This is all for the fact of privacy. If you feel the need to have settings like this, you can easily change it in your privacy settings page.

Why do I get redirected to a "Suspended Account" page?

If you are trying to visit a users profile page, and you get redirected to a "Suspended Account" page, that means that the users account has been disabled for violating our terms or guidelines.

Why do I keep getting logged out?

When you are log in, cookies are set into your browser to authenticate that you are allowed to access the given account you are logging into. After a day or two, these cookies expire/delete. Which is why you will be logged out.
